How to Return Plusnet Router


Thank you for contacting Plusnet today.

As part of your account closure or equipment return process, you can Return Router free of charge via the Post Office.

Here’s how:

• Just go to our Royal Mail page to create a free returns label by clicking on this link https://www.royalmail.com/track-my-return#/details/2886

• You can either print your label at home or use QR code on the label to print it at a Post Office.

• Take the package to your nearest Post Office.

• Keep your proof of postage until we confirm receipt, which usually takes about 7 days.
